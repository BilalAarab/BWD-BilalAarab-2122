About Cupping Therapie

Each cupping therapy can be applied to specific acupuncture points. These methods are good for both physical and emotional complaints. Because the relationship between the earth and the human body is proportional, it is best not to do hijama on special days, such as dry cupping or moxibustion cupping. These days are determined by the ebb and flow of the moon and/or the influence of the human body.
